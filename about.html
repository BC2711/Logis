Frequently Asked Questions

The ClassicGreenCard is the universal fuel card introduced by SpeedPay that allows you to pay for fuel and other products at any service station across the country.

To use the ClassicGreenCard, simply load funds from your central account, visit your nearest service station, inform the attendant that you're using the ClassicGreenCard, and they will deduct the requested gas amount using a POS (Point of Sale) Machine. You'll receive a receipt as confirmation, and your vehicle will be fueled.

You can find a list of networked service stations that accept the ClassicGreenCard on our website www.speedpayzm.com.

To obtain a ClassicGreenCard, you'll need to provide identification, a certificate of incorporation (if you're registering as a business) and complete a KYC (Know Your Customer) registration form.

You can use your ClassicGreenCard to purchase fuel, lubricants, and make payments at some toll gates.

Restrictions may vary depending on the card type. Management/Individualized cards have no restrictions. Fleet cards, provided by organizations, may have specific limitations on fuel types, lubricant purchases, locations, purchase dates, and more.

You will have access to a platform and a mobile app that will allow you to monitor transactions, track expenses, manage your account, and download your statement. The app can also be used as a payment channel.

The card is currently priced at ZMW 100. Access to the system and the mobile application is free of charge.

Currently, the primary benefit is convenience, with a reliable and accurate platform for tracking your spending.

Corporates can also manage their fleet's consumption 24/7, increasing accountability. Additionally, corporates may also receive occasional discounts and loyalty points. Note that there are no loyalty programs, cashbacks, rewards, special offers, or discounts available at this time.
